Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is a simple italian pasta dish that originates from Rome. With just a few simple ingrediants and proper technique, anyone can make Carbonara. Although its simple to make, its flavor and richness will blow you away. This is a cheap dish that wont break the bank and will full you up!

Ingrediants

  • 5L Water
  • Pinch of salt
  • 300g Spaghetti
  • 150g Guanciale
  • 200g Pecorino Cheese
  • 4 eggs
  • Pepper
