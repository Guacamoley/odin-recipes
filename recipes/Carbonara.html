Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is a simple italian pasta dish that originates from Rome. With just a few simple ingrediants and proper technique, anyone can make Carbonara. Although its simple to make, its flavor and richness will blow you away. This is a cheap dish that wont break the bank and will full you up!

Ingredients

  • 5L Water
  • Pinch of salt
  • 300g Spaghetti
  • 150g Guanciale
  • 200g Pecorino Cheese
  • 4 eggs
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil water to cook pasta
  2. Cook pasta to al dente
  3. While pasta is cooking, fry guanciale until crispy
  4. While everything is cooking, we will prepare the sauce
  5. In a bowl, combine eggs, pecorino, and pepper then set aside
  6. Once pasta is done cooking, immediately bring pasta into pan where guanciale is
  7. Turn off pan to avoid overcooking guanciale and pasta
  8. Bring a scoop of pasta water into sauce mixture and combine well
  9. Turn heat back to low, and pour another scoop of pasta water into the pan
  10. Pour sauce mixture of the noodles and mix well
  11. Keep mixing until sauce thickens up
  12. Once thickened, remove noodles onto plate and serve.
