Chicken Katsu

Description

Chicken katsu, also known as panko chicken, or tori katsu is a Japanese dish of fried chicken made with panko bread crumbs which is also popular in Australia, Hawaii, London, California, and other areas of the world

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg beaten
  • 1 cup panko bread crumbs
  • 1 cup frying oil

Steps

  1. Season both sides of chicken with salt and pepper
  2. Place flour, egg, and bread crumbs in separate dishes
  3. Coat chicken in flour, and shake off excess
  4. Dip chicken into egg, then press into bread crumbs on both sides
  5. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat
  6. Place chicken in oil and cook 3 to 4 minutes per side until golden brown
  7. Let chicken rest and then serve
