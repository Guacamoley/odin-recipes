Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat. It is often eaten by itself or as an accompaniment to another dish.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup frozen green peas
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 clove garlic minced
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 cups day-old rice
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil

Steps

  1. Place carrots in a small saucepan
  2. Bring to low boil and cook for 3 to 5 minutes
  3. Heat a wok or stainless steel pan over high heat
  4. Pour oil into pan and stir fry carrots, peas, garlic for 30 seconds
  5. Add eggs and quickly scramble
  6. Stir in rice and soy sauce and toss
  7. Finally drizzle sesame oil over and toss
  8. Serve with anything and enjoy
